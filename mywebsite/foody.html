Foody - Food delivery app and website


Foody is an app and website designed for food delivery, where users can choose from different types of restaurants according to their needs and preferable diets

Project Overview

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand who are primary users of the app. The primary users identified to be working adults as well as students. The majority of users have busy schedules that leave the unable to cook and prepare meals themselves.
